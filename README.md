## Task Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
Created a one-page infographic using **Canva** covering five core digital literacy topics: definition, useful student tools, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette. The design uses a professional blue-and-orange colour scheme suitable for a student audience.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Set up professional profiles on **GitHub**, **LinkedIn**, and **Kaggle**. Created a GitHub profile README, filled in the LinkedIn Education section, and explored Kaggle's datasets and competitions. All platforms will be actively maintained throughout the four-year degree.

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Completed the **"Solve Me First"** challenge on HackerRank using Python. Built a **5-question Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz** on Google Forms (link below), connected to a Google Sheet for response tracking.

🔗 **Google Form Link (Digital Literacy Quiz):** (https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSedpoO052XSWBm2AaH-gnfmENr0vIHErcqJbCszR4M5AcKITg/viewform?usp=header)

### Task 4 – Email Etiquette
Drafted two professional emails — one requesting a deadline extension from a professor, and one expressing interest in a summer internship. Also created a comprehensive social media **Do's and Don'ts checklist** with 7 tips in each category.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Researched **UPI / Online Payment Fraud** and wrote a 240-word realistic case study ("The Refund Scam"). Created a 10-point **Stay Safe Online** checklist with specific UPI safety tips and reporting channels including **cybercrime.gov.in** and helpline **1930**.
